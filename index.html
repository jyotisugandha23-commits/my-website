Our Modules

STEP 01

Dashboard

The dashboard provides a centralized overview of all school activities in real time. It displays key information such as student strength, attendance status, fee summaries, and recent updates.

📊
STEP 02

Teacher Module

The Teacher Module helps educators manage their daily academic activities efficiently. It allows teachers to mark attendance, update student performance, manage timetables, and share assignments or announcements.

👩‍🏫
STEP 03

Student Module

The Student Module provides a complete view of academic and personal information in one place. Students can check their attendance, timetable, assignments, exam results, and important announcements easily.

👨‍🎓
STEP 04

Attendance

The Attendance Module enables quick and accurate tracking of daily student attendance. It provides real-time records, absentee reports, and attendance summaries for better monitoring.

📅
STEP 05

Time Table

The Timetable Module helps schools organize and manage class schedules efficiently. It provides a clear view of subject timings, teacher allocations, and daily routines for students and staff.

⏰
STEP 06

Marks

The Marks Module allows teachers to enter and manage student exam scores quickly and accurately. It generates detailed report cards, performance analysis, and progress summaries.

📝
STEP 07

Fees

The Fees Module helps schools manage fee collection and payment records efficiently. It tracks paid and pending fees, generates receipts, and provides clear financial reports.

💳
STEP 08

Bus Service

The Transport Module helps schools manage student transportation safely and efficiently. It maintains bus routes, vehicle details, driver information, and student allocations.
